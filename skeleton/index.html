About Us

College is a cornerstone of the American Dream, and most families
do not feel they are making an informed decision about college.


We are working with students, parents, counselors, and schools to make high-quality college readiness accessible to all families.

Our Approach

Student-Centered
This process is about you. We
are here to guide you, but you
will be in the driver's seat.
Data-Driven
Our advice is backed by
research and conversations
with admissions officers.
Comprehensive
No cap on hours. We'll work
with you until you feel ready
to hit submit.
Growth-Oriented
The college process is an
inflection point and we push
you to reflect & grow from it.
